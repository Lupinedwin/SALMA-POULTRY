About Salma Poultry

At Salma Poultry, we are dedicated to delivering the best in poultry farming. Our mission is to provide high-quality chicken, fresh eggs, and healthy chicks to meet your needs.

  • Fresh and healthy chicken raised in a sustainable environment.
  • Farm-fresh eggs packed with nutrition and quality.
  • Well-cared-for chicks bred for health and vitality, ensuring the success of your poultry farming ventures.

With a commitment to excellence and a passion for poultry farming, Salma Poultry is your trusted partner in providing fresh and healthy poultry products. We believe in sustainable practices and delivering quality you can trust.

You can place your order with us today, and we will deliver countrywide. Whether you're looking for chickens, eggs, or chicks, we have got you covered. Our products start as low as 1300 Kenyan Shillings per chicken.

We are conveniently located in Koru, and we are ready to serve you with top-notch poultry products and services.
